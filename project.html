Who we are ?

01 CLUB: is an active group of engineering students whose common objective is to boost the IT events scene, improve the personal and professional skills of students by programming several activities (training, workshops and competitions, etc.). The club also marked its presence by the organization of "TECH EXPERIENCE" which is the largest IT event in the north of the Kingdom, organized at ENSAH - National School of Applied Sciences of Al Hoceima.
